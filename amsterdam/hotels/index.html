Hotels

From the classic to the modern, Amsterdam has a wide variety of hotels – 370 in all. While you are free to pick a hotel of your choosing, let us make a few suggestions. We have secured rooms in three hotels: Inntel Hotel Amsterdam, Renaissance Amsterdam Hotel and DoubleTree by Hilton.

The Inntel Hotel

The Inntel Hotel in Amsterdam Centre is our main conference hotel. We selected the Inntel for its cozy Amsterdam atmosphere. This four star hotel has 239 rooms with luxurious bathroom, satellite TV and Wi-Fi. It’s a five minute walk from Amsterdam Central Station.

Room price is 166 euro per night, including breakfast, Wi-Fi and taxes.
These rates are valid until January 15, 2014 for overnight stays between February 3 – 9. Reservations must be confirmed by providing a valid credit card for guarantee. Cancelation is free of charge until 15:00 CEST, one day before arrival. In case of late cancellation or no show, the first night will be charged.

Amsterdam is easy to get around

The center of Amsterdam is compact. You can walk from one end to the other in about an hour. Driving is not recommended unless you are familiar with the strict traffic restrictions.

Luckily Amsterdam has an excellent public transport system: trams, buses, metro and trains. By far, the most Dutch option is to rent a bike!

An excellent online information source, covering everything from bus and train connections to city metro lines, is 9292ov.nl »
