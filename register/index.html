Workshops

The capacity of each workshop is limited to 25 participants. Tickets include lunch and bus transfer Amsterdam – Hilversum.

Tickets include transport, lunch, a guided museum tour and a few beers. Field Trips have a minimum of 16, and a maximum of 24 participants.

Conference tickets

The conference takes place from Thursday February 6 through Saturday February 8.

Conference – Students € 230 sold out
Conference – Single day ticket € 230 sold out

All prices are in euro. No VAT applicable.

Regular tickets include entrance to the conference and all social events (Welcome Party, UX in the City, Amsterdam Journeys, Interaction Awards Celebration), lunch and refreshments. Conference tickets do not include workshop admission.

Social events tickets give access to the Welcome Party on Wednesday and the Interaction Awards Celebration on Saturday.

Payment methods

You can pay online with Visa, MasterCard or American Express.

Go green

Climate change is one of the biggest challenges of our time. When buying tickets you can opt to compensate for the CO2 emissions created by your travel to the event and your stay at the event. Your contribution will support a climate protection project in Mozambique.

Cancellation Policy

Only delegates who are refused a travel visa will receive a refund. Refunds are subject to a 5 euro transaction fee. Any other cancellation requests will not be eligible for a refund, however you can transfer your registration to another person at no cost.

Visa for the Netherlands

If you hold a passport from a country which requires a visa to visit the Netherlands, we can supply you with a Letter of Invitation. Here’s what you need to do:

  1. Register and pay for your conference pass/workshop first – unfortunately we cannot give you a Letter of Invitation in advance (don’t worry, we’ll give you a refund if your visa is refused).
  2. Email us at interaction@ixda.org with the following details:
    • Your confirmation number
    • Your full name
    • Date of birth
    • Address
    • Phone number

We will then send you an official Letter of Invitation which you can then use to support your visa application.

Please contact interaction@ixda.org if you have any questions about the registration.
