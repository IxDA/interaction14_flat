Increase your visibility

As a community, we have always prided ourselves on crafting great conference experiences that people talk about year after year. Right now, the working committee of Interaction14 is planning an inspiring four days and we need your help.

We are looking for companies to sponsor or exhibit at Interaction14. Our conferences are a good way to reach decision makers in the interaction design community. So if your company wants to sell your services, create mindshare or recruit employees, this is the place.

How many people will you reach?

Based on previous attendance figures, we estimate that 800 people will join us in Amsterdam. About 50% of the attendees will be from North America, 45% from Europe and 5% from the rest of the world. Our attendees are spread equally across levels of experience. They include VPs of Fortune 1000 companies, interaction designers, information architects, product managers, students and academic staff. There is an even split between those who work inside client corporations and those who work for consultancies and agencies.
